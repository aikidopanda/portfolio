About Me

My name Alexey Soroker. I am 34, I am from Moscow, Russia. My first education is historian. I studied in historical department of Higher School of Economics in Moscow and worked as private teacher in history, helping school and university students to study and to prepare for their exams. Since my hobby is computer games, once I started to create mods for them. During several years I became more and more involved in that. That's how I found that coding can be interesting and exciting.

In October 2022 I moved to Israel, currently I live in Haifa. After I moved, I could no longer do what I did in Russia. To my mind, teacher's work is based on eye-to-eye contact and doing it online is much less effective. That's why In March 2023 I entered the DI courses in Ramat Gan and finished it in July of the same year. I am eager to find my first job in Israeli high-tech and get necessary experience in this industry, because technical hard skills seem necessary to react to rapid and frequent changes that happen in the world.

HoMM-inspired Card Game

Being a gamer since I was a teenager, I have been wondered how is it to build your own computer game. So, when I learned python to some extent, I did it via pygame module. This project is a card duel game, inspired by Heroes of Might and Magic game series. It currently has 4 factions with different playstyle, similar to original game factions. There are 28 cards in the game. Most of them have their unique abilities, from simple ones (like ranged/flying unit behaviour) to complicated synergies. The core game mechanics have been tested and balanced.

Later I used python web-sockets to bring the game into multiplayer. You can find the link to a server file as well, if you want to create your own server. One of the github links below also contains full guide to all cards and factions, currently presented in the game.

The work on this project is still in progress, cause I am going to add new factions, new cards and some GUI improvements.
